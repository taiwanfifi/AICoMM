## PART 3: RECENT HOT TOPICS AND KEY PAPERS (2024-2026)

### TOPIC 1: Semantic Communications

**Overview:** Semantic communication (SemCom) goes beyond Shannon's paradigm by transmitting the "meaning" of information rather than raw bits, using deep learning for semantic encoding/decoding. This is one of the hottest research directions toward 6G.

**Key Papers:**

1. **C. Chaccour, W. Saad, M. Debbah, Z. Han, and H. V. Poor, "Less Data, More Knowledge: Building Next-Generation Semantic Communication Networks,"** IEEE Communications Surveys & Tutorials, vol. 26, no. 2, pp. 1302-1345, 2024.
   - *Contribution:* Proposes a transformative vision shifting from data-centric AI-augmented systems to reasoning-driven AI-native frameworks underpinned by causal reasoning and transfer learning. Introduces novel semantic languages with minimalist representations and redefines performance metrics to surpass Shannon's bounds.

2. **Z. Lu, R. Li, K. Lu, X. Chen, E. Hossain, Z. Zhao, and H. Zhang, "Semantics-Empowered Communications: A Tutorial-cum-Survey,"** IEEE Communications Surveys & Tutorials, vol. 26, no. 1, pp. 41-79, 2024.
   - *Contribution:* Comprehensive tutorial covering semantic communication fundamentals, architectures, and key enabling techniques from an information-theoretic and deep learning perspective.

3. **Y. Guo et al., "A Survey on Semantic Communication Networks: Architecture, Security, and Privacy,"** IEEE Communications Surveys & Tutorials, vol. 27, no. 5, pp. 2860-2894, 2025.
   - *Contribution:* Addresses the critical but underexplored security and privacy challenges in semantic communication networks, providing a taxonomy of threats and countermeasures.

---

### TOPIC 2: AI-Native Air Interface / 6G

**Overview:** The vision for 6G includes an AI-native air interface where ML models replace or augment traditional signal processing blocks (modulation, coding, detection, etc.).

**Key Papers:**

4. **T. J. O'Shea and J. Hoydis, "An Introduction to Deep Learning for the Physical Layer," (Foundational)** IEEE Transactions on Cognitive Communications and Networking, vol. 3, no. 4, pp. 563-575, 2017. *(Foundational reference; very highly cited)*
   - *Contribution:* Pioneering work proposing autoencoders for end-to-end learning of communications systems, treating transmitter-channel-receiver as a single DNN.

5. **"Way to Build Native AI-Driven 6G Air Interface: Principles, Roadmap, and Outlook,"** IEEE Transactions on Network Science and Engineering, 2024.
   - *Contribution:* Proposes a native AI-driven air interface architecture built around two core characteristics: compression and adaptation. Provides roadmap for 6G AI-native PHY design.

6. **"A Comprehensive Review of AI-Native 6G: Integrating Semantic Communications, Reconfigurable Intelligent Surfaces, and Edge Intelligence for Next-Generation Connectivity,"** Frontiers in Communications and Networks, 2025.
   - *Contribution:* Spans reputable databases focusing on works published 2020-2025, providing unified review of AI-native 6G integrating SemCom, RIS, and edge intelligence.

---

### TOPIC 3: Deep Learning for Channel Estimation and Detection

**Overview:** DL-based channel estimation and signal detection for MIMO-OFDM systems has become a mature research area, with transformer-based and generative model approaches emerging.

**Key Papers:**

7. **M. Luan and J. Thompson, "Channelformer: Attention Based Neural Solution for Wireless Channel Estimation and Effective Online Training,"** IEEE Transactions on Wireless Communications, vol. 72, no. 8, 2023.
   - *Contribution:* Proposes a hybrid encoder-decoder architecture exploiting attention/transformer mechanisms for channel estimation. Demonstrates superior performance over CNN-based methods.

8. **J. Guo, Y. Lv, C. Wen, X. Li, and S. Jin, "Learning-Based Integrated CSI Feedback and Localization in Massive MIMO,"** IEEE Transactions on Wireless Communications, October 2024.
   - *Contribution:* Joint deep learning framework for CSI feedback and user localization in massive MIMO, demonstrating multi-task learning benefits.

9. **X. Zhou et al., "Generative Diffusion Models for High Dimensional Channel Estimation,"** IEEE Transactions on Wireless Communications, vol. 24, no. 7, pp. 5840-5854, July 2025.
   - *Contribution:* Applies score-based generative diffusion models to high-dimensional MIMO channel estimation, showing that generative priors can significantly improve estimation accuracy.

---

### TOPIC 4: Reinforcement Learning for Resource Allocation

**Overview:** Deep reinforcement learning (DRL) enables dynamic, real-time resource allocation in complex wireless networks including network slicing, power control, and spectrum management.

**Key Papers:**

10. **"Deep Reinforcement Learning for Online Resource Allocation in Network Slicing,"** IEEE Transactions on Mobile Computing, vol. 23, no. 6, pp. 7099-7116, 2024.
    - *Contribution:* Proposes online DRL-based resource allocation for network slicing that adapts to dynamic traffic without prior knowledge of arrival patterns.

11. **S. Ebrahimi et al., "Resource Management From Single-Domain 5G to End-to-End 6G Network Slicing: A Survey,"** IEEE Communications Surveys & Tutorials, October 2024.
    - *Contribution:* Comprehensive survey covering evolution of AI-driven resource management from 5G to 6G, including DRL, multi-agent RL, and federated approaches.

12. **"Flexible RAN Slicing in Open RAN With Constrained Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning,"** IEEE Journal on Selected Areas in Communications, February 2024.
    - *Contribution:* Addresses O-RAN intelligent resource management using constrained multi-agent RL to handle inter-slice resource allocation with QoS guarantees.

### TOPIC 5: Large Language Models (LLMs) for Networking

**Overview:** An emerging and rapidly growing research area exploring how pre-trained LLMs can be adapted for network optimization, management, and telecom operations.

**Key Papers:**

13. **D. Wu, X. Wang, Y. Qiao, Z. Wang, J. Jiang, S. Cui, and F. Wang, "NetLLM: Adapting Large Language Models for Networking,"** ACM SIGCOMM 2024.
    - *Contribution:* First framework providing coherent design to harness LLM capabilities for networking. Demonstrates LLM-adapted solutions for viewport prediction, adaptive bitrate streaming, and cluster job scheduling that outperform state-of-the-art algorithms.

14. **A. Maatouk, N. Piovesan, F. Ayed, A. De Domenico, and M. Debbah, "Large Language Models for Telecom: Forthcoming Impact on the Industry,"** IEEE Communications Magazine, 2024.
    - *Contribution:* Examines LLM inner workings for telecom applications including anomaly resolution and technical specification comprehension. Identifies essential research directions for LLMs in the telecom domain.

15. **H. Zhou et al., "Large Language Model (LLM) for Telecommunications: A Comprehensive Survey on Principles, Key Techniques, and Opportunities,"** IEEE Communications Surveys & Tutorials, 2024.
    - *Contribution:* Presents LLM fundamentals (architecture, pre-training, fine-tuning, inference) and introduces LLM-enabled key techniques for telecom in generation, classification, optimization, and prediction problems.

### TOPIC 6: Federated Learning in Wireless Networks

**Overview:** Federated learning (FL) enables distributed model training across wireless devices while preserving data privacy, with over-the-air computation being a key enabler.

**Key Papers:**

16. **"A Comprehensive Survey on Communication-Efficient Federated Learning in Mobile Edge Environments,"** IEEE Communications Surveys & Tutorials, 2025.
    - *Contribution:* Addresses the major bottleneck of communication overhead in FL over wireless networks, surveying compression, quantization, and sparsification techniques.

17. **"Fair Resource Allocation for Hierarchical Federated Edge Learning,"** IEEE Journal on Selected Areas in Communications, vol. 42, November 2024.
    - *Contribution:* Addresses fairness in federated learning across hierarchical edge networks including space-air-ground integrated networks.

18. **"Over-the-Air Federated Learning via Weighted Aggregation,"** IEEE Transactions on Signal Processing, 2024.
    - *Contribution:* Proposes weighted over-the-air aggregation scheme that exploits the superposition property of wireless channels for efficient FL model updates, with provable convergence guarantees.

### TOPIC 7: Digital Twins for Networks

**Overview:** Network digital twins create virtual replicas of physical networks for simulation, optimization, and predictive management, increasingly powered by AI.

**Key Papers:**

19. **"Network Digital Twin Toward Networking, Telecommunications, and Traffic Engineering: A Survey,"** IEEE Access / IEEE Xplore, 2025.
    - *Contribution:* Comprehensive survey covering NDT architectures, enabling technologies, and applications across networking, telecommunications, and traffic engineering domains.

20. **"Coverage Optimization for Large-Scale Mobile Networks With Digital Twin and Multi-Agent Reinforcement Learning,"** IEEE Transactions on Wireless Communications, 2024.
    - *Contribution:* Proposes digital twin-assisted multi-agent RL for coverage optimization in large-scale mobile networks, demonstrating the synergy between DT simulation and DRL decision-making.

21. **"Generative AI Empowered Network Digital Twins: Architecture, Technologies, and Applications,"** ACM Computing Surveys, 2025.
    - *Contribution:* Examines how generative AI (GANs, diffusion models, LLMs) can enhance network digital twins for more accurate simulation and prediction.

### TOPIC 8: Generative AI for Communications

**Overview:** Generative models (GANs, VAEs, diffusion models) are being applied to channel estimation, signal generation, network design, and semantic communications.

**Key Papers:**

22. **"CDDM: Channel Denoising Diffusion Models for Wireless Semantic Communications,"** IEEE Transactions on Wireless Communications, March 2024.
    - *Contribution:* Proposes channel denoising diffusion models (CDDM) adapted to wireless channel models for semantic communications. Proves CDDM can effectively reduce conditional entropy of received signals.

23. **"AI-Generated Network Design: A Diffusion Model-Based Learning Approach,"** IEEE Network Magazine, 2024.
    - *Contribution:* Applies diffusion models to automatically generate customized network topology and configuration solutions.

24. **"Generative AI for the Optimization of Next-Generation Wireless Networks: Basics, State-of-the-Art, and Open Challenges,"** arXiv:2405.17454, 2024.
    - *Contribution:* Reviews generative AI models (GANs, Transformers, diffusion models) and communication paradigms for 6G, exploring how generative AI improves resource allocation and network performance.

### TOPIC 9: Deep Joint Source-Channel Coding (DeepJSCC)

**Overview:** Deep learning enables joint optimization of source and channel coding, outperforming separate coding schemes especially at low SNR and short blocklengths.

**Key Papers:**

25. **E. Bourtsoulatze, D. B. Kurka, and D. Gunduz, "Deep Joint Source-Channel Coding for Wireless Image Transmission,"** IEEE Transactions on Cognitive Communications and Networking, vol. 5, no. 3, pp. 567-579, 2019. *(Foundational work; very highly cited)*
    - *Contribution:* First to apply DL-based JSCC for wireless image transmission using CNN autoencoders with non-trainable channel layer. Foundational DeepJSCC framework.

26. **D. Huang, F. Yuan et al., "D2-JSCC: Digital Deep Joint Source-Channel Coding for Semantic Communications,"** IEEE Journal on Selected Areas in Communications, 2025.
    - *Contribution:* Proposes digital deep JSCC framework bridging the gap between analog DeepJSCC and practical digital communication systems.

27. **D. B. Kurka and D. Gunduz, "DeepJSCC-f: Deep Joint Source-Channel Coding of Images With Feedback,"** IEEE Journal on Selected Areas in Information Theory, vol. 1, no. 1, pp. 178-193, 2020.
    - *Contribution:* Extends DeepJSCC with channel output feedback mechanism inspired by the Schalkwijk-Kailath scheme, improving progressive image transmission quality.

28. **"Deep Joint Source-Channel Coding for Adaptive Image Transmission Over MIMO Channels,"** IEEE Transactions on Wireless Communications, 2024.
    - *Contribution:* Extends DeepJSCC to MIMO channels using vision transformers (ViT), proposing DeepJSCC-MIMO for adaptive spatial multiplexing.

### TOPIC 10: Integrated Sensing and Communications (ISAC)

**Overview:** ISAC unifies radar sensing and wireless communications into a single system, sharing spectrum, hardware, and waveforms. A key enabler for 6G.

**Key Papers:**

29. **F. Liu, C. Masouros, A. P. Petropulu, H. Griffiths, and L. Hanzo, "Joint Radar and Communication Design: Applications, State-of-the-Art, and the Road Ahead,"** IEEE Transactions on Communications, vol. 68, no. 6, pp. 3834-3862, 2020. *(Highly cited foundational survey)*
    - *Contribution:* Comprehensive survey of radar-communication coexistence and dual-function radar-communication (DFRC) systems.

30. **F. Liu et al., "Reshaping the ISAC Tradeoff Under OFDM Signaling: A Probabilistic Constellation Shaping Approach,"** IEEE Transactions on Signal Processing, vol. 72, pp. 4782-4797, 2024.
    - *Contribution:* Introduces probabilistic constellation shaping to reshape the fundamental sensing-communication tradeoff under OFDM signaling.

31. **"Intelligent Integrated Sensing and Communication: A Survey,"** Science China Information Sciences, 2025.
    - *Contribution:* Comprehensive overview of intelligent ISAC covering motivation, applications, AI methods for ISAC, key techniques, and future research issues.

32. **"Communication-Assisted Sensing in 6G Networks,"** IEEE Journal on Selected Areas in Communications, 2025.
    - *Contribution:* Explores how communication signals and infrastructure can be leveraged to enhance sensing capabilities in 6G networks.

---

### TOPIC 11: Reconfigurable Intelligent Surfaces (RIS) with AI

**Overview:** RIS consists of programmable metasurface elements that can reshape the wireless propagation environment. AI/ML is critical for optimizing RIS phase shifts.

**Key Papers:**

33. **C. Huang et al., "Multi-Hop RIS-Empowered Terahertz Communications: A DRL-Based Hybrid Beamforming Design,"** IEEE Journal on Selected Areas in Communications, vol. 39, no. 6, 2021. *(Highly cited)*
    - *Contribution:* Proposes DRL-based hybrid beamforming for multi-hop RIS-assisted THz communications, addressing joint digital and analog beamforming complexity.

34. **"Energy Efficiency in RSMA-Enhanced Active RIS-Aided Quantized Downlink Systems,"** IEEE Journal on Selected Areas in Communications, 2025.
    - *Contribution:* Combines rate-splitting multiple access (RSMA) with active RIS, using DRL for energy efficiency optimization in practical quantized systems.

35. **"Empowering ISAC Systems With Federated Learning: A Focus on Satellite and RIS-Enhanced Terrestrial Integrated Networks,"** IEEE Transactions on Wireless Communications, 2024.
    - *Contribution:* Integrates federated deep RL for joint beamforming and RIS phase-shift optimization in satellite-terrestrial ISAC networks.

36. **"A Comprehensive Survey on Reconfigurable Intelligent Surfaces (RIS) and STAR-RIS for Next-Generation Wireless Networks,"** Discover Applied Sciences, 2025.
    - *Contribution:* In-depth review of RIS and STAR-RIS technologies covering beamforming, channel estimation, electromagnetic information theory, and AI-driven optimization.

**Key Papers:**

37. **"Deep Learning Frameworks for Cognitive Radio Networks: Review and Open Research Challenges,"** arXiv:2410.23949, 2024.
    - *Contribution:* Reviews deep learning techniques for spectrum sensing, signal classification, and dynamic spectrum access in cognitive radio networks.

38. **"Machine Learning-Based Spectrum Occupancy Prediction: A Comprehensive Survey,"** Frontiers in Communications and Networks, 2025.

39. **"Multi-User Opportunistic Spectrum Access for Cognitive Radio Networks Based on Multi-Head Self-Attention and Multi-Agent Deep Reinforcement Learning,"** Sensors, vol. 25, no. 7, 2025.

### TOPIC 13: Edge Intelligence / Edge AI

**Overview:** Deploying AI at the network edge for real-time inference, with research on model partitioning, split inference, and communication-efficient training.

**Key Papers:**

40. **Z. Zhou, X. Chen, E. Li, L. Zeng, K. Luo, and J. Zhang, "Edge Intelligence: Paving the Last Mile of Artificial Intelligence With Edge Computing,"** Proceedings of the IEEE, vol. 107, no. 8, pp. 1738-1762, 2019. *(Foundational; highly cited)*
    - *Contribution:* Comprehensive survey covering architectures, frameworks, and key technologies for DL training and inference at the network edge. Defines the edge intelligence taxonomy.

41. **"Empowering Edge Intelligence: A Comprehensive Survey on On-Device AI Models,"** ACM Computing Surveys, 2025.
    - *Contribution:* Explores current state, technical challenges, and future trends of deploying AI models on edge and terminal devices.

42. **"Optimal AI Model Splitting and Resource Allocation for Device-Edge Co-Inference in Multi-User Wireless Sensing Systems,"** IEEE Transactions on Wireless Communications, 2024.
    - *Contribution:* Addresses joint optimization of DNN model splitting point and wireless resource allocation for multi-user device-edge co-inference.

43. **"A Survey on Trustworthy Edge Intelligence: From Security and Reliability to Transparency and Sustainability,"** IEEE Communications Surveys & Tutorials, 2024.
    - *Contribution:* Addresses the critical trustworthiness aspects (security, privacy, fairness, robustness) of edge intelligence systems.

### TOPIC 14: Foundation Models for Wireless Communications (Emerging)

**Overview:** An emerging frontier applying foundation models (large pre-trained models) to wireless physical layer tasks, aiming for generalization across diverse channel conditions and tasks.

**Key Papers:**

44. **"Towards a Wireless Physical-Layer Foundation Model: Challenges and Strategies,"** IEEE ICC 2024 Workshop.
    - *Contribution:* Proposes a unified wireless physical-layer foundation model (WPFM) with strategic framework for embedding wireless time series, self-supervised pre-training, and semantic representation learning.

45. **"Integrating Pre-Trained Language Model With Physical Layer Communications,"** IEEE Transactions on Wireless Communications, 2024.
    - *Contribution:* Explores how pre-trained language models can be adapted for physical layer communication tasks including channel estimation and signal detection.

46. **"Federated Fine-Tuning for Pre-Trained Foundation Models Over Wireless Networks,"** IEEE Transactions on Communications, 2025.
    - *Contribution:* Addresses efficient fine-tuning of large pre-trained models in distributed wireless settings using federated learning.

### TOPIC 15: Model-Based Deep Learning for Communications

**Overview:** Combining domain knowledge (communication models) with deep learning through techniques like deep unfolding/unrolling to achieve interpretable and data-efficient AI.

**Key Papers:**

47. **N. Shlezinger, J. Whang, Y. C. Eldar, and A. G. Dimakis, "Model-Based Deep Learning,"** Foundations and Trends in Signal Processing, vol. 17, no. 4, pp. 291-416, 2023.
    - *Contribution:* Tutorial-style presentation of model-based deep learning methodologies lying in the middle ground between classical optimization and pure data-driven DL. Covers deep unfolding for communications applications.

48. **"Joint Waveform and Beamforming Design in RIS-ISAC Systems: A Model-Driven Learning Approach,"** arXiv:2502.14325, 2025.
    - *Contribution:* Applies model-driven deep learning (deep unfolding) to joint waveform and beamforming optimization in RIS-assisted ISAC systems.

### TOPIC 16: Graph Neural Networks for Wireless Networks

**Overview:** GNNs capture the graph structure of wireless networks (interference graphs, topology) for scalable and generalizable resource allocation.

**Key Papers:**

49. **"Graph Neural Networks for Wireless Networks: Graph Representation, Architecture and Evaluation,"** IEEE Transactions on Machine Learning in Communications and Networking, 2024.
    - *Contribution:* Provides comprehensive framework for applying GNNs to wireless networks, covering graph representations, GNN architectures, and evaluation methodologies.

50. **"A Survey of Graph-Based Resource Management in Wireless Networks,"** University of Waterloo / IEEE, 2025.
    - *Contribution:* Surveys algorithm unfolding as an effective bridge between GNNs and traditional optimization (WMMSE, SCA), showing scalability to wireless networks of various sizes.

### TOPIC 17: Over-the-Air Computation for Distributed Learning

**Key Papers:**

51. **"IRS Assisted Federated Learning: A Broadband Over-the-Air Aggregation Approach,"** IEEE Transactions on Wireless Communications, 2024.
    - *Contribution:* Leverages intelligent reflecting surfaces (IRS) to combat wireless fading and noise in broadband over-the-air federated learning.

52. **"Asynchronous Federated Learning via Over-the-Air Computation in LEO Satellite Networks,"** IEEE Transactions on Wireless Communications, 2024.
    - *Contribution:* Extends over-the-air FL to non-terrestrial networks using LEO satellites, addressing asynchronous aggregation challenges.

### TOPIC 18: Transformer/Attention Architectures for Communications

**Overview:** Transformer architectures are being adapted from NLP/vision to wireless communications for channel estimation, signal detection, and end-to-end system design.

**Key Papers:**

53. **"Channelformer: Attention Based Neural Solution for Wireless Channel Estimation and Effective Online Training,"** IEEE Transactions on Wireless Communications, 2023.
    - *Contribution:* Hybrid encoder-decoder with transformer encoder for channel estimation in OFDM systems. Demonstrates effective online training and adaptation.

54. **"Joint Channel Estimation and Feedback With Masked Token Transformers in Massive MIMO Systems,"** Scientific Reports, 2025.
